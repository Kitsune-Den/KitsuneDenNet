// commands

Everything is a slash command.

Grouped by what they do. No prefixes to remember.

Playback

/play · /search

Paste a link to queue instantly, or search by name and pick from results.

/playnext · /playskip

Jump a track to the front of the queue, or skip straight to it.

/skip · /pause · /resume · /stop

The basics, exactly where you'd expect them.

/replay · /forward · /rewind

Restart the current track, or jump forward/back a few seconds.

Queue

/queue · /nowplaying

See what's up next, and what's playing right now.

/shuffle · /loop · /skipto

Reorder, repeat, or jump to a position in the queue.

/remove · /removedupes · /clear

Clean up the queue without stopping what's playing.

/addprevious · /grab

Re-queue the last track, or DM yourself a link to the current one.

Sound

/volume

Free servers cap at 100%; Premium goes up to 200%.

/filter

bassboost, nightcore, vaporwave, karaoke, lowpass, and more — Premium.

/seek

Jump to any position in the current track — Premium.

Always on

/247

Keep Lyra connected around the clock — Premium.

/autoplay

Related tracks keep playing once the queue runs dry.

/join · /leave

Bring Lyra into your channel, or send her away.

Premium

/premium subscribe · status

Check the tier, or get a link to upgrade a server.

/premium activate · deactivate

Assign or free up a premium slot for this server.

Info

/help · /about

Browse commands, or learn what Lyra is.

/ping · /invite

Check latency, or grab the invite link.

// faq

Good to know.

A plain search term (Lyra shows you five results to pick from), or a direct YouTube, Spotify, or SoundCloud link — tracks, albums, and playlists all work.
750 tracks per server, and playlists load the first 6 tracks free. Premium removes both caps entirely.
One subscription grants a number of server slots (1 for Prelude, 3 for Sonata, 6 for Symphony). Assign or free up a slot per server anytime with /premium activate and /premium deactivate.
No. Lyra only plays audio, she never records or transcribes anything in your voice channel.
