What Echo does

Drop it in a server, hop in voice, and start talking — or typing.

🔊

Reads chat aloud

Auto-joins your voice channel when people gather and speaks new messages, then leaves when it's empty.

🗣️

A voice per person

Everyone can pick their own voice with /myvoice, so the channel sounds like real people talking.

♿

Accessibility built in

Captions voice activity and live-transcribes speech to a text channel — free on every server.

🔗

Smart announcements

Calls out links, GIFs, and attachments, skips code blocks and spoilers, and reads names instead of IDs.

🗂️

Custom pronunciations

Teach Echo how to say names and acronyms with /pronounce, per server.

✨

Premium voices

Upgrade a server to ultra-realistic ElevenLabs voices — same commands, richer sound.

Six voices to choose from

Commands

Slash commands, grouped by what they do.

CommandWhat it does
/join · /leaveBring Echo into your voice channel, or send it away.
/saySpeak a one-off message in your voice channel.
/autoread on · offAuto-read a text channel aloud (voice channels' built-in chat works automatically).
/voice set · list · current · resetChoose the server's voice from the six curated picks.
/myvoice set · current · resetPick your own voice — your messages get read in it.
/pronounce add · remove · listTeach Echo custom pronunciations for names and acronyms.
/accessibility on · off · statusCaption voice activity (joins, leaves, mutes) to a text channel.
/accessibility transcribeLive speech-to-text — captions what people say in voice.
/premium status · subscribe · manageCheck the tier, upgrade the server, or manage billing.
/usageSee this month's usage against the server's allowance.

Built for everyone

Echo works both directions, so no one's left out of the conversation:

It reads text aloud for members who'd rather listen, and captions & transcribes voice into text for hearing-impaired members. Accessibility is free on every server, regardless of tier — and live transcription runs on our own hardware, so speech audio never goes to a third party.
