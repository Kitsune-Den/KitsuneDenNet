// what echo does

Drop it in, hop in voice, and go.

No setup wizard, no dashboard. Echo gets to work the moment people gather, whether typing or talking.

🔊

Reads chat aloud

Auto-joins your voice channel when people gather and speaks new messages, then quietly leaves when it's empty.

🗣️

A voice per person

Everyone picks their own voice with /myvoice, so the channel sounds like real people talking.

♿

Accessibility built in

Captions voice activity and live-transcribes speech to a text channel, free on every server, every tier.

🔗

Smart announcements

Calls out links, GIFs, and attachments, skips code blocks and spoilers, and reads names instead of raw IDs.

🗂️

Custom pronunciations

Teach Echo how to say names and acronyms with /pronounce, per server.

✨

Premium voices

Upgrade a server to ultra-realistic ElevenLabs voices: same commands, the same six picks, richer sound.

♿ the part that matters most

Built to work both directions.

Echo reads text aloud for members who'd rather listen, and captions & transcribes voice into text for hearing-impaired members. No one sits out the conversation.

✓

Free on every server. Accessibility is never gated behind a tier. It's there from the first install.

🔒

Stays on our hardware. Live transcription runs on our own machines, and speech audio never goes to a third party.

// six voices to choose from

Pick a voice. Hear it.

Set the server's voice with /voice set, or claim your own with /myvoice set.

Click any voice to preview. Free servers use natural local voices · Premium unlocks the ElevenLabs versions.

// commands

Everything is a slash command.

Grouped by what they do. No prefixes to remember.

Basics

/join · /leave

Bring Echo into your voice channel, or send it away.

/say

Speak a one-off message in your voice channel.

Reading

/autoread on · off

Auto-read a text channel aloud (voice channels' built-in chat works automatically).

/pronounce add · remove · list

Teach Echo custom pronunciations for names and acronyms.

Voices

/voice set · list · current · reset

Choose the server's voice from the six curated picks.

/myvoice set · current · reset

Pick your own voice. Your messages get read in it.

Accessibility

/accessibility on · off · status

Caption voice activity (joins, leaves, mutes) to a text channel.

/accessibility transcribe

Live speech-to-text that captions what people say in voice.

Premium & usage

/premium status · subscribe · manage

Check the tier, upgrade the server, or manage billing.

/usage

See this month's usage against the server's allowance.
